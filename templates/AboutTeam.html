Hi, We are FIRST Tech Challenge Team 7159, the RoboRavens. We operate out of Canyon Crest Academy to take part in the San Diego First Tech Challenge competition. We are made up of students from many different grade levels, so we are constantly joined by new members, and as an organization, we are very sustainable.

The team is currently ran by two copresidents, William Lin and Helen Cho. For the actual robots, the head of Mechanical is Benjamin Clifner and the head of controls is William Lin.
